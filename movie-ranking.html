The Best Movies According to Thato

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

This is a movie about humanity's sacrifices to survive. It's about how pioneers are willing to lay down their own lives so that an entire species might go on.

Arrival

Arrival explores the meaning of language and communication, in the context of how to communicate with extraterrestrials who arrived on Earth before tensions lead to war

Se7en

Se7en is about 2 detectives, who are hunting a serial killer who uses the seven deadly sins as his motives.
